the sequence is similar to fibonacci sequence but instead of being 0 1 1 2 3..... it is 0 1 2 3 5 8.....

for n number of of squares in a hopscotch board, the user will
a) consider all n-1 scotch boards and add one square at the end.
b) consider all n-2 scotch baords and add two simultaneous squares at the end of all the boards.

sum of both will give you possible number of boards.
